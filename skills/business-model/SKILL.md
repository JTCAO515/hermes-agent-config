# Business Model Design

Three canvas formats for designing and analyzing business models. Use the right one for the stage:

- **Business Model Canvas**: full business model (existing or planned businesses)
- **Lean Canvas**: early-stage startups (problem/solution fit)
- **Startup Canvas**: combines product strategy with execution roadmap

## A. Business Model Canvas

Nine building blocks: Customer Segments, Value Propositions, Channels, Customer Relationships, Revenue Streams, Key Resources, Key Activities, Key Partnerships, Cost Structure. For each: clear description, current state assessment, and key risks.

## B. Lean Canvas

Adapted for early-stage startups: Problem, Solution, Key Metrics, Unique Value Proposition, Unfair Advantage, Channels, Customer Segments, Cost Structure, Revenue Streams. Focus on risk reduction and assumption testing.

## C. Startup Canvas

Combines Product Strategy (vision, target customer, needs, value proposition, offerings) with execution (capabilities, features, business model, OKRs, metrics). More comprehensive than Lean Canvas; bridges discovery and delivery.
