# Market Analysis

Three complementary market analysis methods.

---

## A. Market Segments

Identify 3-5 potential customer segments with demographic, psychographic, and behavioral profiles. For each: size estimate, needs assessment, willingness to pay, and channel strategy.

## B. Market Sizing

Estimate market size using TAM (Total Addressable Market), SAM (Serviceable Available Market), and SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market):
- **Top-down**: industry reports, analyst data, published market sizes, growth rates
- **Bottom-up**: unit economics (price x volume), customer counts, penetration rates
- **Value theory**: willingness-to-pay surveys, conjoint analysis, price sensitivity

Always provide both top-down and bottom-up estimates with source citations. Show assumptions and sensitivity.

## C. Marketing Ideas

Generate 5 creative, cost-effective marketing ideas with:
- Target segment and channel
- Estimated cost and reach
- Success metrics (CPL, CPA, conversion rate, ROAS)
- Implementation timeline
- Key risks and dependencies
