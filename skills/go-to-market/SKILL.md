# Go-To-Market Strategy

Two stages: first identify the right GTM motions, then build the full strategy.

---

## Phase A: GTM Motions

Identify the best GTM motions and tools across these motion types:
1. **Product-led growth**: freemium, free trial, product-qualified leads
2. **Sales-led**: outbound SDR, field sales, enterprise account management
3. **Content-led**: SEO, blog, educational content, thought leadership
4. **Community-led**: forums, user groups, events, advocates
5. **Partner-led**: channel partners, resellers, integrations, marketplace
6. **Event-led**: conferences, webinars, workshops, sponsored events
7. **Hybrid**: combinations of the above

For each applicable motion: target segment, expected CAC, LTV, cycle time, and tools needed.

## Phase B: Full GTM Plan

Create a comprehensive go-to-market strategy covering:
- **Target segments and buyer personas** (from market analysis)
- **Value proposition and positioning** (from product positioning)
- **Channels and motions** (from Phase A)
- **Pricing and packaging strategy**
- **Marketing strategy and campaigns**
- **Sales process and enablement**
- **Launch plan with timeline and milestones**
- **Success metrics and OKRs**
- **Budget and resource requirements**
