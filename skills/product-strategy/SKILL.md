# Product Strategy

Three product definition tools.

## A. Product Vision

Brainstorm an inspiring, achievable, and emotional product vision statement. Format: "For [target customer] who [need], our [product] is a [category] that [key benefit]. Unlike [competitor], we [differentiator]." Include: target audience, unmet need, core offering, key benefit, competitive alternative, differentiator, emotional hook.

## B. Product Strategy (9-Section Framework)

1. **Vision**: where we're going
2. **Target Customer**: who we serve
3. **Needs**: what problems we solve
4. **Value Proposition**: why they choose us
5. **Offerings**: what we build
6. **Capabilities**: our competitive advantages
7. **Features & Roadmap**: how we deliver
8. **Business Model**: how we sustain
9. **OKRs & Metrics**: how we measure

## C. Product Name

Brainstorm 5 unique, memorable product names with: name, rationale (why this name), associations (what it evokes), availability (domain/ trademark check), and pronunciation guide.
