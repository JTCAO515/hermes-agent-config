# Deal Workflow

Five phases of the PE deal lifecycle.

---

## A. Deal Screening

Quickly screen inbound deal flow — CIMs, teasers, and broker materials. Determine whether a deal warrants further attention based on:
- Strategic fit with fund mandate
- Financial profile (revenue, growth, margins, EBITDA)
- Industry thesis alignment
- Key risks and red flags
- Initial valuation range

Output: one-page screening memo with go/no-go recommendation.

## B. Deal Sourcing

Discover target companies for proactive outreach. Channels:
- Industry databases and screening tools
- M&A databases
- Industry conferences and events
- Management and board networks
- Sector research and market maps
- Add-on acquisition targets for existing portfolio companies

Output: target company list with rationale and contact strategy.

## C. Deal Tracker

Track multiple live deals through milestones:
- Deal name, company, sector
- Current stage (sourcing, screening, LOI, diligence, execution)
- Key deadlines and dates
- Deal team and responsibilities
- Key metrics (revenue, EBITDA, EV, multiple)
- Next steps and action items
- Status (active, paused, dropped, closed)

Output: tracker table or spreadsheet suitable for weekly deal meetings.

## D. Due Diligence Checklist

Generate and track comprehensive due diligence checklists:
- Commercial: market, competitive position, customer concentration, pipeline
- Financial: quality of earnings, working capital, debt, tax, capex
- Legal: contracts, IP, litigation, regulatory, employment
- Operational: facilities, systems, supply chain, insurance
- Management: backgrounds, references, retention risk

Output: checklist with status per item, responsible party, and key findings.

## E. DD Meeting Prep

Prepare for due diligence meetings with management. For each session:
- Meeting objective and agenda
- Management background (CVs, previous meetings notes)
- Key topics and data requests
- Questions by topic area (with follow-up probes)
- Red flags to probe
- Action items tracker

Output: meeting prep memo for each session.
