# Competitive Analysis

Three levels of competitive intelligence.

## A. Competitor Analysis

Build a structured competitive landscape: list key competitors, market positioning, product offerings, target segments, pricing, strengths, weaknesses, and strategic moves. Sources: web search, user feedback, product analysis, financial filings.

## B. Competitive Battlecard

Create sales-ready battlecards: compare your product vs specific competitors across feature sets, pricing, target use cases, and buyer personas. Include objection handling scripts, competitive positioning statements, and win/loss analysis. Output: one-pager per competitor suitable for sales enablement.

## C. Competitive Landscape Scan

High-level competitive landscape overview for strategic planning. Covers market map, competitor tiering, recent funding/M&A, and key trends. Use for quarterly reviews and strategic planning.
