# Customer Insight

Three complementary methods for understanding customers.

## A. Ideal Customer Profile

Identify the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) from research data. Determine: company size, role/function, industry, tech stack, pain points, budget authority, and buying process. Distinguish ICP from personas: ICP is who to sell to, personas explain how they buy.

## B. Customer Journey Map

Create an end-to-end customer journey map with stages (awareness, consideration, decision, onboarding, retention, advocacy), touchpoints, customer emotions, pain points, and opportunities. For each stage: what the customer is doing, thinking, feeling, and what the business can improve.

## C. Value Proposition

Design a detailed value proposition using a 6-part JTBD framework: customer segment, job to be done, desired outcomes, pain points, current alternatives, and our unique solution. Then generate value proposition statements suitable for marketing, sales, and product positioning.
